About the MathStarsCMR

The site name MathStarsCMR Brazil was chosen to represent Brazilian students who excel in national and regional mathematics competitions such as OBM, OBMEP, and OMU. The term "MathStars" conveys the idea of excellence, talent, and prominence-young stars shining through mathematics. The word "Brazil" provides the geographic and national context for the project. Optional domain availability: mathstarscmrbrazil.eb.mil.br.

The Mathematics Olympiad is an extension activity carried out, in general, by the Mathematics Department of Federal Universities (UFs) with the support of the Brazilian Mathematics Olympiad Association (AOBM) and STONE, together with other Higher Education Institutions (IES).

The Brazilian Mathematics Society organized the first Mathematics Olympiad (OBM) in 1979. Over the years, OBM has undergone several changes in its format, but maintained the central idea, which is to encourage the study of Mathematics in students, develop and improve teacher training, influence the improvement of teaching, in addition to discovering young people talented.

The Brazilian Mathematics Olympiad (OBM) is a program of the Brazilian Mathematics Society (SBM) and the Brazilian Mathematics Olympiad Association (AOBM). In addition to holding the annual edition of the Olympics throughout Brazil, the OBM organizing committee is responsible for selecting and training Brazilian teams that participate in international competitions, such as the International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO), Ibero-American Mathematics Olympiad (IMO) and International Mathematics Competition for University Students (IMC).

Medals - This year, OBMEP multiplied the number of medals for those registered. 650 gold, 1,950 silver and 5,850 bronze medals will be distributed to participants from public and private units. Students who win these medals will be invited to participate in the Jr. Scientific Initiation Program (PIC) as an incentive and promotion of academic development.

This guide have the intention to help students to find some of the most known mathematics olympics in Northeast of Brazil, and, of course, some of the most important mathematics olympics in Brazil, like, OMU and OBM. Some links of them will be presented in this guide
